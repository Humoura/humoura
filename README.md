A modern, humor-based social media platform designed for connection and positivity. Share laughs, connect with friends, and enjoy a community built on humor and kindness.

🌐 **Live Demo:** [humoura.vercel.app](https://humoura.vercel.app)

## ✨ Key Features

### 🎬 Content Sharing
- **Feed** - Share and view humorous posts from your connections
- **Vines** - Browse a beautiful grid of uplifting and humorous content
- **Like & Comment System** - Real-time interactions with automatic count updates
- **Positivity Filter** - Content moderation to keep the platform positive and welcoming

### 💬 Direct Messaging
- **Connection-Based Messaging** - Message only users you follow for privacy
- **Video Sharing** - Send videos directly to connections
- **Real-time Updates** - Instant message delivery

### 👤 User Profiles
- **Profile Customization** - Upload profile pictures and create a personalized bio
- **Follow System** - Build connections and expand your network
- **User Settings** - Complete control over privacy, notifications, and preferences
- **Profile Stats** - Track your posts, followers, and following

### ⚙️ Settings & Privacy
- **Profile Settings** - Manage visibility and bio
- **Privacy Controls** - Public profile, messaging permissions, and post visibility
- **Notification Preferences** - Customize what you're notified about
- **Account Management** - Change password and account control

### 👑 Admin Panel
- **User Management** - View and manage all platform users
- **User Statistics** - Track platform metrics
- **System Overview** - Monitor platform health
